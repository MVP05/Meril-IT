üìÑ Introduction

This document outlines the structure, roles, and functional usage of Zoho Expense for Meril Life Sciences Pvt. Ltd., supporting transparent, multi-tiered, and policy-compliant expense workflows.

üß¨ About Meril

Meril Life Sciences Pvt. Ltd. is a global medical device company committed to the design, development, and manufacturing of novel, clinically relevant, and state-of-the-art devices. Founded in 2006, Meril has subsidiaries such as:

  • Meril Diagnostics Pvt. Ltd. (MDPL)
  • Meril Endo-Surgery Pvt. Ltd. (MEPL)
  • Meril Healthcare Pvt. Ltd. (MHPL)
  • Meril Life Sciences Pvt. Ltd. (MLSPL)
  • Meril Life Sciences India Pvt. Ltd. (MLSIPL)
  • Micro Life Sciences Pvt. Ltd.

Objective of Document

To provide a comprehensive understanding of:

  • Customized workflows across each Meril company
  • Expense policies and approval hierarchy
  • Integration points with other systems
  • Audit compliance and user training
